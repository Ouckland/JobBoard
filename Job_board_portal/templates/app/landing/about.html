Our Story

Founded in 2024, JobBoard emerged from a simple observation: traditional job platforms were becoming increasingly complex, impersonal, and inefficient for both job seekers and employers. We set out to build a solution that prioritizes human connection while leveraging technology to streamline the hiring process.

What started as a university project has evolved into a comprehensive recruitment platform trusted by startups, SMEs, and established corporations alike. Our commitment to continuous innovation and user-centric design has driven our growth and shaped our vision for the future of work.
